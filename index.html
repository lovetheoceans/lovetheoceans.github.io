Plastics in the Ocean

And why they are bad


    Ever wonder why animal population rates are dropping by so much? It's because the fish are dying. The sea covers ~70% of the earth's surface, and 78% of animal biomass lives in there, it is incredibly important we protect it. An estimated 20 percent of the human population get a large amount of their protein from the sea, but yet we are pouring tonnes and tonnes of plastic into the ocean.
